## SDG Justification

Our Student Project & Milestone Tracker advances SDG 4: Quality Education (Target 4.4) by digitizing capstone project management with structured guide allotment, evaluation workflows, and publication tracking - enabling students to develop industry-aligned software engineering practices. The CSV export and certificate management (CO4) support SDG 16: Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions (Target 16.6) by providing transparent, filterable progress reports and verifiable publication records for institutional accountability. Built with Django's validated forms (CO2) and sequential approval logic, the system ensures equitable access (SDG 4.5) for all student teams while promoting transparent, auditable academic processes aligned with outcome-based education goals.
